About

Expert Gunsmithing, Built on Veteran Pride.

Black Goat Gunsmithing is owned and operated by Keith Gautreaux, a proud disabled Army veteran with a passion for the Second Amendment and honest, high-quality work. Drawing on his military experience, Keith approaches every project with the same discipline, precision, and dedication that shaped his service.

At Black Goat Gunsmithing, we believe in putting customers first, delivering quick turnarounds, and tailoring each build to your unique vision. That’s why we offer one-on-one consultations, detailed estimates, and a truly collaborative design process—ensuring every modification reflects your preferences and needs.

FAQ

Straight-Shooting Answers to Your Most Asked Questions.

Q: How long does a typical modification or restoration take?

A: Turnaround times vary based on the complexity of the work and parts availability. We strive to offer a quick turnaround and will give you an estimated timeline once we know your project details.

Q: What are your prices?

A: All pricing is case by case, as each firearm and modification has unique factors—from parts and materials to the complexity of the work. We’ll discuss all costs during our one-on-one consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

Q: Are there any laws or regulations I need to be aware of?

A: All firearm modifications must comply with federal and state regulations—particularly regarding barrel length, trigger work, and other factors that could classify a firearm differently. We always ensure our work meets legal standards, but it’s ultimately your responsibility to follow all applicable laws. If you have concerns about a specific modification, feel free to discuss it with us before we begin.

Q: How do I get started with a custom build or modifications?

A: Just reach out via our contact form or phone number. We’ll set up a consultation to discuss your vision, provide a detailed estimate, and plan the project timeline.

Q: Where are you located?

A: We’re based in Ocean Springs, Mississippi.

Q: Do you offer shipping or out-of-state services?

A: Yes! We do offer out-of-state orders, for more info give us a call!
